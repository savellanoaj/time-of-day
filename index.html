Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by AJ Savellano

Greeting goes here

This page changes its image, custom message, and background color based to whatever the time of the day is. It is seperated using if else statements that determine the current hour of the day by a getHours method.

For example, between 5am and 12 noon, the page will show a picture of a doll that has a sun on it, a saying that says 'Good Morning!' and the color orange for the background of the page.

I also added what the day of the week it is. For extra credit ;).
